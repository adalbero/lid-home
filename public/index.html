The Test

Leben in Deutshland Test or Einbürgerungstest is a test prepared by Bundesamt für Migration und Flüchtlinge (BAMF).

The test consists of 33 questions. 30 questions from a catalog of 300 general purpose questions, plus 3 out of 10 specific questions from your Bundesland.

Each question has 4 options, which only one is correct.

You have 60 minutes to complete the test. To pass, you need to get at least half of the questions right (17 of 33).

The App

The app is designed to offer an easy and interactive way to prepare yourself for the test. It uses the official catalog of questions and implements a set of cool features to help you be more productive in your study.

All questions of the catalog are grouped into categories and smart lists. You can flag questions with your custom tags, like "difficult" or just search for a specific word, like "DDR". The App can even read out loud the question to you. You can also translate any question to your native language.

It keeps track of your progress and generates statistics making it easy to see where your weak points are. You can exercise only the questions you got wrong in the past

You can also simulate the test with 33 random questions. Or exercise with 20 new questions every time.

Find more details about the app features in the App page.
