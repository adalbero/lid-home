The Test

Leben in Deutshland Test or Einbürgerungstest consists of 33 questions. To pass, you need get at least half of the questions right (17 of 33). You will have 60 minutes to complete the test.

The test will pick 30 question from a catalog of 300 general propose questions, plus 3 out of 10 specific to your Bundesland.

Each question has 4 options, which only one is correct.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide tools and information to everybody that wants to succeed in the test.

We have organized the catalog of questions from Bundesamt für Migration und Flüchtlinge (BAMF) in easy way that you can prepare yourself for the test.

The App

The app is designed to offer an easy and interactive way to prepare yourself for the test.

With this App you can organize your study for the test. It is organized by topics and area of interest. You can select your Bundesland, flag some questions with your custom tags, like "difficult" or just search for a specific word, like "DDR". The App can even read out loud the question to you. You can also translate any question to your native language.

It keeps track of your progress and generate statistics making it easy to see where your weak points are. You can exercise on only questions you got wrong in the past.

You can also simulate the test with 33 random questions. Or exercise with 20 new questions every time.

Good luck in the test!
